How Nadia Works

Interactive Trainings

Engaging, hands-on trainings that help educators understand the function of behavior, identify patterns and root causes, and apply effective, relationship centered strategies. Offered in both English and Spanish because at Nadia, we believe educators deserve access to trainings they truly understand.

Program Level Consulting

Nadia partners directly with early education programs to implement Nadia’s behavior support framework, build systems for data tracking and behavior support plans, strengthen teacher family communication, and develop sustainable, equitable practices where exclusion is the last resort and inclusive environments are the standard.

Behavior Support Strategies

Nadia will provide step by step behavior strategies that help educators understand what behavior is communicating, recognize patterns and function, and choose the most effective response in the moment.

Why Programs Choose Nadia

Nadia was created to change how early childhood programs respond to behavior. Instead of reacting to challenges, we help educators understand them. Through interactive trainings, program level consulting, and step by step strategies, Nadia builds confident teams, inclusive environments, and sustainable practices that support every child and every educator.
